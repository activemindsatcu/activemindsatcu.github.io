About Us

We are Columbia University's chapter of Active Minds Inc., a national nonprofit organization that empowers students to speak openly about mental health in order to educate others and encourage help-seeking.

Our Mission

  • Increase our peers' awareness of mental health issues
  • Provide information and resources regarding mental health and mental illness
  • Encourage our peers to seek help as soon as it is needed
  • Serve as a liaison between fellow students and the mental health community
  • Remove the stigma that surrounds mental health issues
  • Create a comfortable environment for an open conversation about mental health issues
